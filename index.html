Fashion of San Francisco

Streetwear:

San Francisco's Streetwear really encompasses a "throwback" generation. Right now Gen X is having a surge of new clothing inpired by 90s Tv shows. Where as the Millenials have been loving the neutrals and muted colors. Moreover, there is a Hype Beast movement in the fashion industry where young people are buying extremely expensive brands and combining them into neutral outfits.

Financial District:

Generally the basic outfit of a man in the financial district is a fashionable Armani suit with a subtle navy or black tie. Women on the other hand have a larger spectrum in fashion for downtown. Since there is such a crazy weather pattern in the Bay Area women usually opt for some items that compliment well when layered.
